Welcome and thank you for downloading IanniX!

IanniX is a graphical open-source sequencer, based on Iannis Xenakis works, for digital art. IanniX syncs via Open Sound Control (OSC) events and curves to your real-time environment (PureData, C-Sound, SuperCollider, openFramework, Processing, vvvv, Live, Max/MSP/Jitter… and many others from our links section)!

Feel free to suscribe to our forum. Post your problems or tips to make live our community!
